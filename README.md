# Walker Track

Walker Track is a Fire Direction Center tracking application for day-to-day operational management.

It helps teams track:
- unit structure and assignments
- launchers, pods, and ammunition status
- fire missions and tasking
- activity logs and reporting
- network/sync status between connected app copies

## What It Is For

Walker Track is designed for fast, practical tracking during planning, training, and live coordination.  
The app keeps your operational picture in one place so teams can see readiness, assignments, and updates quickly.

## Key Areas In The App

- **Dashboard:** high-level operational snapshot
- **Inventory:** munitions and equipment status
- **Management:** hierarchy and personnel/equipment relationships
- **Fire Missions:** mission tracking and updates
- **Logs:** event history and action trail
- **Network/Simulation:** synchronization and optional simulation workflows
- **Settings/System Info:** configuration and device context
